How it works

The system stores your review, analyzes its main category, predicts the opinion, and makes the result available in the dashboard.

  • Write a short and clear review.
  • Select a rating from 1 to 5 stars.
  • The analysis runs automatically after submission.
  • Authorized users can view results in the dashboard.

Example categories include Food Quality, Cleanliness, Staff Behavior, and Price.
