How it works

The system stores your review, analyzes it, and sends the result to the dashboard.

  • Write a short review.
  • Select a rating from 1 to 5.
  • The system predicts category and opinion.
  • Results appear in the dashboard.

Example categories: Food Quality, Cleanliness, Staff Behavior, and Price.
